Math Careers Outside Academia

911 Van Vleck - April 14, 2017

A professional development symposium at the University of Wisconsin - Madison, April 14, 2017, focusing on providing graduate students a sense of potential careers outside of academia, and what skills they should be developing to pursue these careers. This is being organized by DJ Bruce, Zachary Charles, Daniel Erman, and Alisha Zachariah.
